    About Us

    TROW Management Ltd. is a private company which deals with refurbishment and treatment of electronic waste. We want the environment to be in it's original state as far as possible so that our future generations can live in a healthy and safe environment. We as a company always look forward in giving employment opportunities and doing the best we could do for the environment. Support us in the noble cause by sending all the unnecessary and unwanted electronic waste to TROW.

    Our Business Model

    • In the first stage, we will contact government organizations and private organizations to make a deal with us and give all their e-waste products to us for its treatment. This will be beneficial for both, the government and us because to date there is no one else who is giving this kind of service.

    • Not only the new waste be generated but we will also look to crack the deal with the government to give us the tender/opportunity to take all the e-waste from the landfills nearby.

    • In return for this, we will give a certain amount of profit to the organization (who so ever wishes to give us the waste), that profit percentage will be decided in the meeting with these organizations.
